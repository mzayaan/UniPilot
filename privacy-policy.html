Privacy Policy

UniPilot – Student Management App

Last updated: 28 June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to UniPilot ("we", "our", or "us"). UniPilot is a mobile application designed to help university students manage modules, tasks, grades, timetables, budgets, and study resources.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using UniPilot you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the app and delete your account.

Our app is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account data: email address, display name, and profile photo when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Academic data: module names, grades, timetable entries, tasks, and deadlines you enter into the app.
  • Financial data: budget categories and spending amounts you enter (Pro plan feature). This data is stored on our servers and is never shared with payment processors.
  • Uploaded files: lecture notes and documents you upload to the app's storage.
  • AI interactions: prompts you send to the AI Study Coach. These are processed via our backend and are not stored beyond the session unless you save the output.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device identifiers: anonymous device ID used by RevenueCat to track subscription status.
  • Crash and performance data: anonymised error reports to help us fix bugs.
  • Push notification tokens: used solely to deliver task reminders you have scheduled.

2.3 Information from third parties

  • Google Sign-In: name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account when you choose this sign-in method.
  • RevenueCat: subscription status, entitlement details, and customer ID used to manage your plan.

4. AI Study Coach

UniPilot's AI Study Coach sends your prompts to a Supabase Edge Function which then calls an external AI language model API (Google Gemini). Your prompts are sent over an encrypted connection. Your prompt text is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the request unless you explicitly save the generated output to your account.

AI API providers may retain prompt data for abuse-prevention purposes according to their own privacy policies. We recommend you do not include sensitive personal information (e.g. financial account numbers, government IDs) in AI prompts.

AI API keys are never exposed to the mobile app. All AI calls are routed through our secure backend.

5. Data Storage and Security

Where your data is stored Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure, hosted on AWS in the EU (West) region. Uploaded files are stored in Supabase Storage with access restricted to your account via Row Level Security policies.

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2+.
  • All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Row Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data — even our own queries cannot return another user's records.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted device storage (Expo SecureStore).
  • We do not log your raw content or academic records in application logs.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at dulmeerzayaan@gmail.com.

7. Subscriptions and Payments

UniPilot offers optional paid plans (Pro and Pro+). All payments are processed by Google Play. UniPilot does not receive or store your payment card details.

RevenueCat is used to verify subscription entitlements. We store your RevenueCat customer ID and subscription status (plan name, expiry date, product ID) in our database to unlock premium features.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Google Play → Subscriptions.

8. Push Notifications

UniPilot requests permission to send push notifications for task reminders you schedule. Notifications are generated locally on your device using Expo Notifications and are not routed through our servers.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app without affecting any other app functionality.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data (most data can be edited directly in the app).
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Data portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at dulmeerzayaan@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

Account deletion: You can delete your account from the Settings screen within the app. This permanently deletes your profile, academic data, uploaded files, and all associated records from our servers.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where we are required to retain records for legal compliance (e.g. billing records retained for 7 years under applicable tax law).

Anonymised, aggregated analytics data (which cannot identify you) may be retained indefinitely for product improvement purposes.

11. International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in the EU. Some of our service providers (Google, Anthropic, RevenueCat) are based in the United States. When data is transferred outside the EU, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards as required by applicable law.

12. Children's Privacy

UniPilot is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Users aged 13–17 should have parental or guardian consent before using the app.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice or email at least 7 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
